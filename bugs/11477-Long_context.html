Long context menu delay

When right-clicking in the editor, it takes annoyingly long (around half a second) to open the context menu. And what makes it much worse, moving the mouse during that time causes the menu to not appear at all.

Tested with the June 26 build (4185) on wx2.8.4 (Linux, unicode), on a 2GHz system that is otherwise fast enough.
