System locks when reloading changed file

Code::Blocks v10.05

Build: Jun 28 2010, 06:31:33 - wx2.8.11 (Linux,unicode) - 64 bit

OS: CentOS 5.2

When a file is open in Code::Blocks, but modified on disk by another application, Code::Blocks presents the "Reload file?" dialog. Frequently, I am unable to respond to the the dialog, or anything else for that matter. The entire system locks and is unresponsive. I am forced to do a hard-boot to recover.

Bug is intermittent, but frequent enough to prevent me from using Code::Blocks, which makes me sad because I love the interface.
