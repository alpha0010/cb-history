Messages pane strange behaviour

When you hide non-empty, currently selected tab on a message pane, its content "gets cloned" to other tabs.

Way to reproduce:

- select some tab on message pane (ie. "Code::Blocks Debug") so it is active, visible tab.

- right click and hide it (ie. select "Hide->Code::Blocks Debug")

- try switch to other tabs - some of them contain content of the tab that has just been hidden.
