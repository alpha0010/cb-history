Removal of spurious zooming methods

Can I suggest removal of the spurious (and largely undocumented) zooming methods for the edit window.

I inadvertently zoomed in by holding down Ctrl and moving the mouse wheel - it took me ages to work out what I had done - and then I only discovered by accident. Surely this is unintended? And due to a default event handler in the underlying wxWidgets class.

I gather from the forum that there are other zooming methods like numeric keypad + and -. Surely the ability to set the font size is sufficient - any other (undocumented) methods are just a nuisance.
