SmartSense request - method variables

When you type a class, then a method, you get a popup from SmartSense that shows you the variables that the method accepts.

When you type in the first variable, it may be a local variable for example, then you get a new SmartSense popup to auto-complete the variable name. Then when you type a comma to enter the next variable for the method, SmartSense should come back and show you the method variables again.
