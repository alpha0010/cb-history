bninefounder

'Edit' menu items shouldnt be in submenu

First off, please understand I love Code::Blocks and recommend it to my friends. I am very grateful for the efforts of the C::B developers.

The only real issue I have with C::B is that the 'Edit' menu items (Cut, Copy, Paste, etc) are hidden in a sub-menu when you right-click in the text-editor. Compared to other IDEs, I feel this is the only disadvantage of C::B.

Can you please provide an option in 'Settings' where you can decide if the edit functions are at the 'first level' or in a sub-menu? I sometimes have problems with hand and arm movement, and having the edit functions readily available would save MUCH time, effort and pain.

Thank you very much! :-D

Will B.
