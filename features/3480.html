Tone for a clean build

One of the MSVC IDE features that I miss is the tone that signifies completion of a successful (warning- and error-free) build. I guess there could be different sounds for Warnings and Errors, but I really like a tone signifying a Clean build. This should probably be an option that could be turned on or off. (In fact, it may be there somewhere already, but I haven't found it.)
