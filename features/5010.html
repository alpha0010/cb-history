Multi-tabbed find-in-files results

I'm working since a long time with KDevelop 3.5 and want to switch to C::B.

One of the most used features of KDevelop is the concept of the multi-tabbed presentation of the find-in-files (fif) results. That means each single fif operation creates a new tab in the fif output pane. You can then click on the several tabs and see the results of that particular fif even several fif's have been done later.

Each tab could have a close possibility like a close button or a menu entry if you right-click the tab. A close all would also be great.

This might be much more intuitive than the existing solution in C::B. I think, a lot of users will love this feature like I do and this will make C::B even more attractive than it is already.

Thanks for the great job you did already!
